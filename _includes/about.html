ABOUT THE ECAD ORDINANCE

The ECAD Ordinance was originally passed in 2009 by the City of Austin in an attempt to reduce energy consumption, educate citizens about the energy efficiency of their homes, and to collect data for creating programs to help Austinites with energy consumption and electricity bills.

If you are selling your home, you must have an energy audit performed if all three of these statements are true*:

1) Your home is inside Austin city limits.

2) Austin Energy is your energy provider.

3) Your home is over 10 years old.
